In order to set up the hoyolab daily check-in claimer, you have to provide us your cookie.

**What is cookie?**
Cookies are the default form of authentication over the majority of Mihoyo APIs.
For authentication, you will need to send two cookies: `ltuid` and `ltoken.` ltuid is your hoyolab UID and ltoken is a unique token used for the actual authentication.

For more information about security & cookie please [read this](https://github.com/thesadru/genshinstats#is-it-possible-that-my-account-can-be-stolen-when-i-login-with-the-cookie).

**1**. Go to hoyolab.com.
**2**. Login to your account.
**3**. Press F12 to open Inspect Mode (ie. Developer Tools).
**4**. Go to Application, Cookies, https://www.hoyolab.com.
**5**. Copy ltuid and ltoken.
